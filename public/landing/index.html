Create a simple CV 

One CV is used for applications to all O-Link job offers. To improve your chances of being hired, include as much detail you can about yourself, your qualifications, certification and work experience.

Post a Job Offer  

Post the details of a job vacancy you are trying to fill. Include the specific details of the job, any minimum requirements (avoid ineligible applications) and an application threshold to manage the number of applications you are willing to consider per vacancy (avoid excess applications).

 Review, Accept, Decline

The add will be visible for a month to the pool of students looking for jobs, who will then apply to fill your vacancy. Employer

  • Who is eligible to use O-Link?

    All individuals that have been registered at a secondary or tertiary education institution. (The app is in a beta phase and jobs are only available in Cape Town. Our expansion plans are laid out and we will be broadening our reach to other provinces soon)

  • Do I have to create a profile to use the O-Link service?

    Yes. Students will be able to browse the available offers without a profile but a profile is required in order to apply to job offers.

  • Does O-Link guarantee me a job?

    No. O-Link gives you access to a wide range of job offers to which you can apply.

  • What does it cost for Students?

    Students can make 2 free applications every day or they can pay:

     

    - R29 to buy 3 further applications which are valid for one week

    - R49 to buy 7 further applications which are valid for one week

    - R99 to buy unlimited applications which are valid for one month.

     

     

  • How do I change my profile information?

    Clicking your profile picture in the top right of your page will take you to the “User Profile” page. Clicking the “Edit” button will allow you to manage your profile information.

     

  • What does it mean when an offer says “Interview Required”?

    These offers have been posted by Employers who require a physical interview prior to employment. The application and selection process is no different but the end result (blue dot) provides Talent with an interview as opposed to a job.

     

  • How do I know if I have been accepted for a job?

    Under your “Current Applications” tab, a yellow dot signals a pending application, a green dot signals provisional acceptance and once the talent has confirmed they are willing to work, a blue dot will signal this confirmed acceptance.

     

  • What happens if I forget my password?

    On the login screen, simply click the “Forgot Password” button and an email will be sent to you with instructions to reset your password.

     

  • Will I get an Employer's contact details?

    Once a Student has been confirmed (blue dot) for either a job or an interview, the Student will be able to email the respective Recruiter under the “My Applications” page.

     

  • Why should I use O-Link?

    One standardized O-Link CV makes applications to a range of jobs easy. Your O-Link rating will reflect your past performance and give weight to your O-Link CV. The platform is interactive and Students are continuously notified about their application status.

     

  • Who is eligible to use O-Link?

    All businesses looking to hire young, enthusiastic, part time staff.

  • Do I have to create a profile to use the O-Link service?

    Yes. Talent will be able to browse the available offers without a profile but a profile is required in order to post jobs for Talent to see.

  • Does O-Link guarantee me staff?

    No. O-Link provides access to a vast pool of potential applicants who are eager for work.

  • What does it cost for Recruiters?

    For the first 3 months of operation, the service will be FREE for Recruiters! A pricing structure will be incorporated and shared to all Recruiters before the free 3 months come to an end.

  • How do i change my profile information?

    Clicking your profile picture in the top right of your page will take you to the “User Profile” page. Clicking the “Edit” button will allow you to manage your profile information.

     

  • Will I get Talent’s contact details?

    Once Talent has been confirmed (blue dot) for either a job or interview, Recruiters will be able to email the respective Talent under the “My Applicants” page.

  • What happens if I forget my password?

    On the login screen, simply click the “Forgot Password” button and an email will be sent to you with instructions to reset your password.

     

  • Why should I use O-Link?

    O-Link’s unique system allows Recruiters to set minimum criteria for applications, ensuring you receive quality applicants able to perform. All applications are in the same format and include a rating based on previous work, making comparisons easy. You will also be able to set an application threshold, controlling the number of applications you receive per available position.
